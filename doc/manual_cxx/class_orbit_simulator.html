Detailed Description

This module simulates the variation of relevant quantities which depend on the position of the satellite within the its orbit.

Author
David Futyan UGE

The module models the rotation of the field of view, the variation of telescope temperature (affecting PSF breathing) and CCD temperature (affecting dark current and gain). The module also outputs the position and velocity of the satellite in its orbit.
