Detailed Description

Generates PSF halo due to scattering and ghosts.

Author
David Futyan UGE

This module generates a halo due to scattering for each PSF in the FOV, according to a simplified analytic model (Peterson), and generates ghost flux uniformly over the CCD according to the position and flux of each star in the FOV
